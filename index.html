The 14/48 Playground

The 14/48 Projects is an organization that thrives on bringing people together in new and exciting ways, building connections, and strengthening our artistic community. Since that's not possible right now in light of our Shelter in Place mandate, we are looking at ways we can support everyone during this difficult time.

So, we are launching The 14/48 Playground. This is a Facebook group where we can collaborate during these troubled times. Write songs. Write a play. Build a prop. Memorize a monologue. It can all start here.
